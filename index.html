How Many People Apply to Come to the U.S.A - 3rd Quarter 2018

The United States of America welcomes tens of thousands of people from around the world to visit, live and work in the U.S. The following graph visualization shows all forms received and processed by the USCIS in the 3rd Quarter 2018. Additionally the visualizationss dig deeper and show how many applications where approved, denied, pending and the type of each application.
